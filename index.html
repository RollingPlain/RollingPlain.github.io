个人简介About Me

吴冠尧，大连理工大学软件学院博士生，导师为 刘日升教授， 合作者为 刘晋源教授。 主要研究方向为多模态图像融合与图像处理。

在 TPAMI、CVPR 等顶级期刊会议发表十余篇论文，其中 6 篇为一作/学生一作， 3 篇入选 ESI 高被引/热点文章，目前引用超过 3000 次。 主持国家自然科学基金青年学生基础研究项目（国自然），入选中国科协青年人才托举工程博士生专项（青托）， 曾获校级学生最高荣誉优秀研究生标兵、JAL 国际奖学金、两次国家奖学金等， 担任多个会议与期刊的审稿人。

Guanyao Wu is a Ph.D. student at the School of Software, Dalian University of Technology, advised by Prof. Risheng Liu and collaborating with Prof. Jinyuan Liu. His research interests include multimodal image fusion and image processing.

He has published more than ten papers in top-tier venues such as TPAMI and CVPR, including six first-author or student first-author papers. Three papers have been selected as ESI Highly Cited or Hot Papers, and his Google Scholar citations exceed 3,000. He leads an NSFC Young Student Basic Research Project (NSFC), was selected for the CAST Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program doctoral track (CAST), and has received honors including DUT Outstanding Graduate Student Pacesetter, JAL International Scholarship, and two National Scholarships.

He also serves as a reviewer for multiple conferences and journals.
